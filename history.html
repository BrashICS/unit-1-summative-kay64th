Charles Babbage's Life and Achievements

Charles Babbage was born on December 26, 1791, in London, England. He is best known for his work on the "Difference Engine" and the "Analytical Engine," which were early forms of mechanical computers.


The Difference Engine was designed to compute mathematical tables, while the Analytical Engine was a more sophisticated machine, capable of performing general calculations.

Though neither machine was completed during his lifetime, his designs paved the way for modern computer science.
